Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 1/3 cups mashed overripe bananas

Instructions

  • Step 1

    Preheat over to 350 degrees F (175 degress C). Lightly grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.

  • Step 2

    In a large bowlc combine flour, baking soda and salt. In a separate bowl, cream togetehr butter and brown sugar. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture;stir just to moisten. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.

  • Step 3

    Bake in preheated oven for 60 to 65 minutes, until a toothpick inserted into center of the loaf comes out clean. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack.
